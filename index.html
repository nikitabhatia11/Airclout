Drawing

☝️One finger (index) — draw, or stamp the selected sticker
✌️Two fingers (index + middle) — drag the whole drawing around

Picking a color

✊Make a fist — opens color selection
☝️Point while selecting — hover over a swatch for a moment to choose it
🖐️Open your palm — confirms the color and goes back to drawing

Modes & extras

🛠️Tools — blackboard, fill mode, and symmetry, all pointable with your finger
🖌️Brush — set brush size (S/M/L/XL)
⌫Eraser — turn eraser on/off and set its size
🪣Fill mode — picking a color floods the whole canvas instead of just the brush
🖍️Blackboard — chalk now matches whatever color you've picked
🪞Symmetry — mirrors every stroke into a mandala pattern
🎬Timelapse — turns your drawing session into a downloadable GIF

Voice commands

🎙️Tap the mic icon in the toolbar to start listening (Chrome only)
↩️Say "undo" to undo your last stroke
↪️Say "redo" to redo it
🗑️Say "clear", then "confirm clear" to wipe the canvas — the extra step stops accidental wipes
